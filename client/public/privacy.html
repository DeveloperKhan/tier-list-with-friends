Privacy Policy

Tier Lists with Friends · Last updated: April 2026

What data we handle

While a voice-channel session is active, the following data is processed:

  • Discord user ID and display name — provided by Discord, held in server memory for the life of the session only.
  • Images you upload — stored in Cloudflare R2 (cloud object storage) for up to 24 hours, then automatically and permanently deleted.
  • Tier list export renders — if you share your tier list result, the exported image is stored in Cloudflare R2 for up to 24 hours, then automatically deleted.
  • Tier list arrangement — held in server memory for the life of the session only.

We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting of any kind. We do not operate any persistent database.

Image storage

Uploaded images and export renders are stored in Cloudflare R2, a cloud object storage service operated by Cloudflare, Inc. Each image is identified by a randomly generated UUID. Images are not listed publicly, but anyone who obtains the UUID can access the image during its 24-hour retention window. A scheduled cleanup job runs every 6 hours and permanently deletes all expired objects.

You should not upload images containing sensitive personal data.

Third parties

Cloudflare, Inc. processes image data as a sub-processor via R2. Their privacy practices are described in the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

Discord, Inc. governs your identity and OAuth tokens. See Discord's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell or share your data with any other third party.

Children

The Activity is available to all Discord users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what Discord's own age-gating permits. Uploaded images are deleted within 24 hours regardless of who uploaded them.
