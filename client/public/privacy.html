Privacy Policy

Tier List with Friends · Last updated: April 2025

We collect nothing.

Tier List with Friends does not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

What happens during a session

While a voice-channel session is active, the following data is held temporarily in server RAM:

  • Your Discord user ID and display name (provided by Discord)
  • Images you upload to the tier list (as base64 data)
  • The tier list arrangement you and other participants create

This data exists only in memory. It is never written to a database, log file, or any storage medium. It is permanently gone when the session ends (all participants leave or the host resets).

Third parties

We do not share any data with third parties. Discord's own Privacy Policy governs how Discord handles your identity and OAuth tokens.

Cookies & tracking

None. The Activity sets no cookies and uses no analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting.

Children

The Activity does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected, there is nothing to delete.
